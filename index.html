About Us

Helping to educate Overseas since 2004

  • 2012-2013

    Our Humble Beginnings

    We’re one among the first agency to gain a Trusted Partner certificate for UKBA. In 2012 ISRA was created to deal with the growing demand for other services like guardianship, sponsorship, university applications and counselling, student educational tours/excursions, group course bookings, group tours and immigration advice.

  • How Can We Help?

    Step By Step Guidance

    You definitely would like to go to the best institution to match your profile and we at ISRA, have set up unique resources to give a step-by-step guidance for the entire process. Our systematized approach and a network with Universities help students who apply through ISRA.

  • Awards/Achievements

    Working Together Brings Honor

    Since its existence from 2012, ISRA has been constantly providing quality and professional guidance to students. With this approach in our work, we have been able to win over the confidence of students, who approach us to fulfill their educational goals. 95 % of our students have been Studying in various Institutions Abroad. Over 90 % of our students are granted Visa for their first choice destination. 80% of our students are from references!

  • Partner With Us

    Why Partner With Us?

    We are always keen to work in partnership and collaboration with other educational organisations. We’re a team who represents a thorough professional caliber, with no personal benefit involved but we find great satisfaction through the smiles of victorious students who made it large overseas. Our single pointed motto is to promote quality overseas education, and we prioritize for student satisfaction.
